My Criteria

Everyday Breakfast

I have started almost everyday with a protein bar for over a year. I prioritize getting breakfast every day, but often it can be difficult to find the time. Protein bars offer something delicious, nutritious, and fast.

Frequent Snack

I am allergic to gluten, so I am often restricted in the foods I can eat. However, I know I can always rely on a protein bar for a quick snack! I often find myself bringing them with me so I don't have to worry about being hungry.

Sports

I play two sports and am a pretty active person. This means that it is important that I correctly fuel myself with an ample amount of protein and nutrience. Protein bars offer me an efficient solution that fit into my busy schedule.
